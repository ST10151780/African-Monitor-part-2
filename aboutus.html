About Us

History

African Monitor was founded in 2006 as an autonomous pan-African organization committed to tracking development pledges and their effects on local communities.

Mission

African Monitor seeks to act as an impartial facilitator for tracking development promises, execution, and effects at the community level, enhancing the representation of African perspectives in the development discussions.

Vision

To witness the African continent swiftly achieving its development potential, where individuals live with dignity, essential needs are met, human rights are respected, and effective governance is established.

Target Audience

African grassroots communities, civil society organizations, youth advocates, policymakers, development collaborators, and entities dedicated to sustainable development.
