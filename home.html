African Monitor

Welcome to African Monitor. This organization was established in 2006 as an independent continental body to monitor development funding.

Our Motivation

African Monitor understands how the poor currently live and survive, hypothesizing that a better understanding of how communities can influence change will improve development outcomes.

Our Vision

An African continent rapidly achieving its development potential, where people live in dignity, in a just society where basic needs are met.
